My Games

DREAMWILLOW

Art Director, Lead Artist, Animator (9.2019 - 12.2019)

DREAMWILLOW is a top-down 2D dungeon crawler where the player is a necromancer that traverses through a dark forest and ressurects their defeated opponents to join them in their escape.

This was a semester-long project by the WolverineSoft Studio, a group of about 30 students in multiple disciplines.

The Legend of Zelda NES: Remake

Programmer, Designer

The Legend of Zelda NES: Remake is exactly as its name suggests: a remake of the original Zelda game by Nintendo. This was programmed solely by me and one other student as an assignment for EECS 494: Introduction to Game Design and Development. All art a nd audio assets were taken from the original game.

If you press "4" while in-game, you will be teleported to a custom level featuring a custom mechanic that is not in the original game. This was designed by my partner and I.

Play the web build of this remake here!

FALLEN KINGDOM: LEGEND OF SQUIRREL

Sole Environment Artist, Lead Animator, Lead Character Artist (10.2018)

FALLEN KINGDOM: Legend of Squirrel is a lighthearted 2D platformer and rhythm game. The player is a flying squirrel who plays a panflute to calm down squirrelkind after it is revealed that their world is, literally, crashing down.

This was a 5-person project for a 48 hour game jam held by WolverineSoft. The theme was "Fall" and the game won 1st place overall, 1st place for best audio, and 1st place for best art.

REVENGE & REFORM

Sole Artist, Designer (7.2019)

REVENGE & REFORM is a lighthearted 2D platformer where the player is a wolverine that has four forms, each with a different set of advantages and disadvantages. The player navigates through different levels by switching between these forms.

This was a 2-person project for a week-long game jam held by WolverineSoft. The theme was "R&R" and the jam was unranked.

FLAY THE FIRE

Sole Artist, Sole Animator, Minor Programmor (11.2019)

Flay The Fire is exactly as its name suggest; a lighthearted, bright Slay-The-Spire-like. The player is a mouse that fights against various silly woodland creatures in order to add more cards to their deck. Battles are won by playing cards against an opponent.

This was a 2-person project for a 48 hour game jam held by WolverineSoft. The theme was "Start Small" and the game won 5th place overall, and 1st place for best art.

10 DEGREES

Lead Artist, Programmer, UI/UX Designer

10 Degrees is a 2D visual novel about multiple characters who have an impace on the environment. The player plants trees in-game, which result in real trees planted through Third Milennium Alliance!

This was a multi-year, multi-platform project by a group called Abaca Games. The primary team contains 4 members. Although the game didn't do very well for a number of reasons, it was a fantastic learning experience.
